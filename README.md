## 🧩 Projects & Ideas

### Nivela — SaaS/ERP for Service Businesses

Nivela is my own SaaS project focused on service-based businesses.

It is designed for companies that need scheduling, customers, services, products, financial control, cash flow, team management and operational clarity.

The initial market focus includes businesses such as barber shops, salons, beauty clinics and recurring appointment-based operations, with a flexible structure that can expand to other service segments.

Main concepts behind Nivela:

- Multi-tenant SaaS architecture
- Service business management
- Scheduling and customer control
- Financial and cash flow routines
- Team and commission workflows
- Product and stock management
- Segment-based experience
- Practical ERP features without the old ERP complexity
